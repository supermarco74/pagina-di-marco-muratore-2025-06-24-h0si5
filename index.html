About Us

Founded in 2010, Marco Muratore Music emerged from a passion for creating unique musical experiences that bridge the gap between classical composition and contemporary entertainment. What began as a small studio experiment has grown into a recognized name in innovative musical expression.

Our mission is to transform the way people experience music by combining technical excellence with emotional storytelling. We believe in the power of music to both entertain and move people, creating memorable experiences that resonate long after the final note has played. Our core values of creativity, authenticity, and musical excellence guide every composition we create.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of music do you compose?

I specialize in both comedic and delicate compositions, ranging from theatrical scores to intimate wedding music. Each piece is carefully crafted to match the specific needs of the project or occasion.

How can I commission a custom piece?

Custom compositions start with a consultation where we discuss your vision, timeline, and requirements. Contact me directly through email to begin the conversation about your project.

Do you perform live at events?

Yes, I am available for live performances at select events. This includes private gatherings, theatrical productions, and corporate functions. Each performance is tailored to your specific needs.

What is your typical timeline for projects?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple arrangements might take 2-3 weeks, while full theatrical scores can take several months. Early booking is recommended for major projects.

Can I license your existing music?

Yes, many of my existing compositions are available for licensing. Rates vary based on usage type and duration. Contact me for a custom licensing quote for your specific needs.
